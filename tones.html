So, what exactly are tone indicators?

Good question, let's look at wikipedia.

A tone indicator is a symbol attached to a sentence or message sent in a textual form, such as over the internet, to explicitly state the intonation or intent of the message, especially when it may be otherwise ambiguous.

Or, a set of letters and symbols (most commonly one or a few letters after a forward-slash) that is put near text on the internet to convey the meaning of joking or anything impossible without saying so.
